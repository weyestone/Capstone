Emergency Care


We are ready to help you 24/7 for your medical emergencies. Our trained team of emergency medicine doctors, specialists, specialty-trained nurses, technicians, pharmacists, care mangagers, and staff are here to work together to provide lifesaving treatments and care. Our goal is to take care of you and your loved ones inside and outside of our facility.

Getting to the ER

If you have a serious or life-threatening emergency, please call 911 right away. Trained professionals will be able to help you as soon as they arrive onsite and will begin your treatment before your arrival to the hospital. You can find our emergency room on the South side of the hospital campus.

If you are needing a higher level of care than we are able to provide, you can rely on our resources and partnerships to get to you the correct experts within the local area. We maintain a 24-hour helipad for your emergent evacuation and inter-facility transport of patients.

What to Expect

As soon as you arrive, we wil make your visit as smooth as possible by evaluating your situation and starting treatment. The severity of your condition will determine the order that you are seen by the emergency room team. After you go home we will continue to support you by contacting your regular physician or helping you find a specialist for your symptoms. If you need to be admitted to a hospital, we will make sure that there is a bed waiting for you.
